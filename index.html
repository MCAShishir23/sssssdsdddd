    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our services

    What industries do you specialize in?
    We work with clients across various industries including healthcare, finance, retail, education, manufacturing, and more. Our team has experience developing solutions tailored to the unique needs of different sectors. We understand that each industry has its own challenges and requirements, and we pride ourselves on our ability to adapt and deliver customized solutions that drive real business value.
    How long does a typical project take?
    Project timelines vary depending on the complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex AI solution could take several months. We follow an agile development methodology that allows for flexibility and regular communication. During the initial consultation, we provide a detailed timeline with key milestones, and we keep our clients updated throughout the development process.
    Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
    Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your solutions continue to perform optimally. Our team is available to address any issues and implement updates as needed. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, and our support services are designed to help your business grow and adapt to changing technological landscapes.
    Can you work with our existing systems?
    Absolutely! We specialize in integrating new solutions with existing systems. Our team has experience working with a wide range of technologies and platforms. We understand the importance of minimizing disruption to your business operations, and we develop integration strategies that are seamless and efficient. Whether you need to connect new automation tools with your current CRM or integrate AI capabilities into your existing applications, we have the expertise to make it happen.
    What makes TS INVO different from other IT companies?
    Our focus on AI and automation sets us apart. We combine technical expertise with business acumen to deliver solutions that not only meet your current needs but also prepare you for future challenges. Our team stays at the forefront of technological advancements, ensuring that our clients benefit from the latest innovations. We take a holistic approach to digital transformation, considering not just the technology but also the people and processes involved.
    How do you ensure the security of our data?
    Security is our top priority. We follow industry best practices for data protection and implement robust security measures across all our solutions. This includes encryption, secure authentication protocols, regular security audits, and compliance with relevant data protection regulations. We also provide training to our team on the latest security practices and stay updated on emerging threats to ensure we can proactively address potential vulnerabilities.
